Activies You can do in Sri lanka

The island country of Sri Lanka is known as the gem of the Indian Ocean. With diverse landscapes and cultures spread across the different regions of Sri Lanka, there really is something for everyone. This list is quite adventurous and involves a few hikes and all things action! The culture and food of Sri Lanka are incredibly unique and will captivate you throughout your adventures.

Hike Little Adams Peak

If you want to enjoy some of the best views in Ella without needing to hike for hours, Little Adam’s Peak is an awesome adventure. It’s best enjoyed during sunrise or sunset and is a must-see in Sri Lanka. It’s one of the top things to do in Sri Lanka if you enjoy hiking! It’s extremely good value considering you can tick off all three attractions in just one day for about $55.

Ride the Kandy to Ella Train

The Kandy to Ella train journey is said to be one of the most scenic, epic train journeys in not only just Sri Lanka but the world. So with a few days to spare in Sri Lanka, you can purchase a ticket for $1.50 and climb aboard for the 5-hour journey. Yes, the train really does cost just $1.50.

Visit Nine Arch Bridge in Ella

The Nine Arch Bridge in Ella is on the Demodara loop and spans 91 meters at a height of 24m. The beautiful nine arches make it a very picturesque spot especially as it is located in a dense jungle and agricultural setting. Behind the railway, a forest is booming, and below, tea leaves are being cultivated. It’s extremely good value considering you can tick off all three attractions in just one day for about $55.
